📰 Latest News 📻

Lookman Hat-Trick Wins Europa Final! 🏆

Ademola Lookman stunned the football world with a historic hat-trick in the Europa League Final against Bayer Leverkusen.

The Nigerian forward opened the scoring with a composed finish, added a thunderous strike for his second, and sealed his hat-trick with a moment of solo brilliance.

Atalanta ran out 3-0 winners in Dublin, claiming their first ever European trophy in style. Lookman's name is now etched into club folklore.

Way to go(al), Retegui 👑

7 goals in 7 matchdays — Retegui is making waves with his clinical finishing! Atalanta fans are buzzing. 🔥

The striker has quickly cemented his place in the squad with key goals in crucial fixtures. His movement off the ball and deadly accuracy have made him a fan favorite already.

If he keeps up this pace, the Golden Boot may not be far away.

De Ketelaere: “Feeling Good!”

Charles De Ketelaere says he feels "good both physically and mentally" after scoring a brace and helping Atalanta to their 11th straight win. 🔵⚫

The Belgian midfielder is enjoying his best form in an Atalanta shirt, showcasing creativity, balance, and vision in the final third.

Coach Gasperini has praised his versatility and consistency, calling him "a key engine in our attack."
